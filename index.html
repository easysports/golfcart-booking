📋 Cart Specs & Rental Overview ▼
Cart Features
  • Rear flip seat with flat storage bed & compartment for gear or groceries
  • Bluetooth stereo system
  • Backup camera
  • Windshield wiper
  • Custom stitched premium seats with adjustable headrests
  • 4-wheel hydraulic disc brakes
  • USB & USB-C charging outlets
  • All DOT safety equipment (LED lights, turn signals, mirrors, horn, seatbelts)
  • Street legal — can be driven at night on any road posted 35 mph or less
Booking Overview
  • Booking is by the night, similar to a hotel
  • Drop-off is between 10:00 AM – 4:00 PM on your first day
  • Need it in the morning? Start your reservation one day earlier
  • Pick-up is between 8:00 AM – 11:00 AM on your last day
  • Need it on your last day? End your reservation one day later
  • A standard 3-prong 110V outlet is required to charge the cart
Rental Requirements
  • Primary renter must be at least 25 years of age
  • All drivers must be at least 21 years of age
  • All drivers must sign the rental agreement and provide a driver's license and auto insurance
  • No driving under the influence of drugs or alcohol
  • All passengers must be seated and belted at all times
  • Do not exceed the cart's stated passenger capacity
  • All normal driving and parking laws apply
